The Learning Hub platform ANOHA ACADEMY

A new learning experience, more modern, more fun and 100% customizable specially designed for you

The Learning Hub is a hub of content and activities. Indeed, it is the only Cegos platform bringing together all the resources made available for training.

You will be able to access:

  • to your training courses
  • to virtual classes
  • to all the trainer's documentation

The tool can be used anywhere and on all devices: tablets, PCs, Macs and mobiles with an internet access network. You are therefore in total autonomy, which allows you to train anywhere and thanks to a tool at your disposal, you will be able to view your progress.

You also have the opportunity to interact with other participants. For those who wish, you can share: photos, email addresses, LinkedIn profiles.
