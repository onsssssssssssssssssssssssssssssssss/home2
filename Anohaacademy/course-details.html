Problem solving methods

Master the tools and adopt effective behaviors

The objectives of the training

Effectively solve a problem. Unite a team around the problem to be solved. Effectively conduct a work session in the classroom or in the field to set up a corrective and preventive action system.

The training program

1 Make the most of progress groups The principles of permanent progress: PDCA; corrective and preventive actions ... Formulation of the problem: what a problem is, an action for improvement; differentiate curative, corrective and preventive. Progress sheets: keep it simple and useful. Rely on working groups to be more efficient. The roles of action pilots, meeting leaders. 2 problem solving methods: The methods : solve problems in a structured way; at each stage: a defined objective, one or more key tools. Choose the problem: use the Pareto chart effectively; the affinity diagram to clarify the situation. Analyze the problem: the QQOQCCP: 7 questions to clarify a problem; the method Is / Is not, in addition. Look for the root causes: work in a structured way in phases; essential tools: brain-storming, 5M, 5 Whys, field tests, tree of causes; research and try out solutions: use creativity techniques, tree diagrams, field tests. Decide on the best solution: using decision matrices and / or efficiency grids. To apply : formalize the actions to be carried out in an action plan; plan with Gantt and Pert charts. Monitor the effectiveness of hot and cold actions: the importance of indicators and field observations; plan the measurements over time. The conditions for success of the method and the associated tools. The presentation of the final results: knowing how to value the work and keep in memory. 3 Facilitate a progress group Preparation for the working session: choose the participants; define the agendas; structure the meeting. The three principles of facilitation for successful problem-solving meetings: production: get the group to produce, encourage creativity, collect ideas; regulation: knowing how to manage the flow of information, involve everyone; summary: organize your reports, prepare for the next meeting. Animation styles. Identify your own mode of animation. Responsibilities shared between the facilitator and the participants Animation tools. Acquire the right reflexes. 4 Driving progress on a daily basis Solve a problem in the field: how to do it fast and to the point; reconcile teamwork and efficiency. Complementary approaches to advance the business. Key points of the Hoshin, Kaizen, Muda, Six sigma, 8D methods.
